My top 3 hindi movies of all time

Kuch Kuch Hota Hei

This is one of my all time favourite romatic hindi movie.Its lives up to its tagline.

3 Idiots

3 Idiots is an all time bollywood blockbuster.The film is a comedy movie with memorable characters and a perplexing storyline and most importantly, a highly motivational movie which shows us to choose the right path in our life.

Zindagi Na Milega Dobara

The theme of the movie is "Live life to the fullest because it happens only once" which is beautifully narrated with flawless writing and perfect characterization.
